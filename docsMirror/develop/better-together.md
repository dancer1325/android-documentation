Android is a growing ecosystem of devices, from phones, tablets, and foldables to laptops, watches, TVs, and more. Better Together focuses on making all these devices work in perfect harmony, creating captivating and seamless experiences for your users. As an Android developer, you have the opportunity to take advantage of the Android ecosystem to engage users more deeply by making your app work intelligently across devices.

## Get started

Whether it's creating apps that are compatible across multiple devices or building rich cross-device experiences, you can enable the right Better Together integrations for your app with the following guides:

  * [About the Continue On feature](/develop/better-together/continue-on): Enable app transitions between Android devices with the Continue On feature.
  * [Multidevice development](/multi-device-development): Design, build, and deliver enhanced user experiences on devices across the Android ecosystem.
  * [Cross device SDK](/guide/topics/connectivity/cross-device-sdk/overview): Create apps that are compatible across multiple devices.
